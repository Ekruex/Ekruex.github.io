The lasagna of lasagnes

Classic Lasagne Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagne sheets
  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 200g grated mozzarella cheese
  • 500ml béchamel sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagne sheets, a layer of ricotta cheese, and a layer of béchamel sauce.
  6. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of béchamel sauce.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Bake for 40-45 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
