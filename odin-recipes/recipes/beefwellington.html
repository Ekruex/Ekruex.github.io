Wellington's Big Juicy Beef

Beef Wellington

Ingredients:

  • 1 beef tenderloin (500g)
  • 200g mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 6 slices prosciutto
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the beef with salt and pepper, then sear in a hot pan. Let cool.
  2. Spread Dijon mustard over the beef.
  3. Sauté mushrooms until moisture evaporates.
  4. Lay out prosciutto on plastic wrap, spread mushrooms on top, and wrap around the beef.
  5. Wrap everything in puff pastry, seal edges, and brush with egg wash.
  6. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Let it rest before slicing!
