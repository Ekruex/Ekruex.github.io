Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 200g plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Sear chicken in a pan, then set aside.
  3. Cook onions, garlic, and canned tomatoes in the same pan.
  4. Add cream and butter, then return chicken to the pan. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve with rice and naan!
